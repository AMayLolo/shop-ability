Shop Ability Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Shop Ability collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Shop Ability mobile app and its related price-scanning services.

Information we access

  • Camera access, so you can scan barcodes, shelf labels, and product pricing.
  • Images you intentionally capture while using the scanner.
  • Product names, prices, categories, and cart details that you enter or confirm in the app.

How we use information

  • Detect barcodes and estimate item pricing.
  • Help you review scanned items and add them to your shopping cart.
  • Provide budgeting, trip-planning, and accessibility-focused shopping features.

How scanning works

When you choose to capture a price label or product image, that image may be sent to our hosted processing service in order to extract pricing and product details. That hosted service may use OpenAI to analyze the image and return product and price information to the app.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with service providers when necessary to operate core app features, such as image-based price extraction.

Data retention

We aim to minimize retained data. Scanner images and extracted results are used to process your request and support app functionality. We may retain limited operational logs for troubleshooting, reliability, and security purposes.

Your choices

You can choose not to grant camera permission, but some scanning features will not work without it. You can also review and edit scanned product information before adding it to your cart.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information used by the app, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children

Shop Ability is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
